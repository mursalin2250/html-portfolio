Website
A website (also written as a web site) is a collection of web pages and related content that is identified by a common domain name and published on at least one web server.
Websites are typically dedicated to a particular topic or purpose, such as news, education, commerce, entertainment or social networking.
Domain
-A domain is essentially your website's address on the internet. It's the unique name that people type into their web browsers to find your site.
Think of it like your home address in the real world. Just like your home has a physical address that lets people know where to find you, your website has a domain name that helps people find your site online.
Hosting
Hosting, in the realm of the internet, refers to the service that provides the storage space and technical infrastructure necessary for your website or web application to be accessible online.
Imagine it as the digital land where your website's virtual house is built. Just like a physical house needs land to stand on, your website needs hosting to exist and be visible on the internet.
HTML
-HTML or Hyper text markup language defines the content and layout of web pages using elements like headings, paragraphs, lists, images, and links.
It serves as the skeleton that gives a website its basic structure and organization.
CSS
-CSS or cascading style sheet styles the appearance of HTML elements, controlling fonts, colors, spacing, layouts, and even animations.
It transforms a plain HTML structure into an aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly experience.
Javascript
-It adds dynamic behavior and interactivity to web pages, making them more engaging and responsive.
JavaScript enables features like animations, form validation, user interactions, and real-time updates.
CMS
-Content Management System (CMS) is the control panel for your website. It's the interface you use to build, edit, and manage your content, like adding text, images, or videos.
Think of it as the captain's helm, guiding your ship's course and making sure everything runs smoothly.
Market Place
-Marketplaces are bustling hubs where you can find additional tools and resources to enhance your website.
From themes and plugins to SEO tools and security services, these online marketplaces offer a treasure trove of options to equip your ship for any adventure.
